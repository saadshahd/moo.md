Use the career skill's Stakeholder Navigator workflow exactly as written in `references/stakeholder.md`.

Gather situation context, your role, deadline, desired outcome, and constraints. Then:
- Distill core issue and tension points
- Map facts, disagreements, and stakeholder matrix (power, interests, levers)
- Develop 3 strategic options with success probability
- Recommend approach with 72-hour action plan
- Include risk mitigation and exit strategy
