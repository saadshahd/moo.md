Use the career skill's Interview Simulator workflow exactly as written in `references/simulate.md`.

Gather role level, domain, and company context via ask tool. Then:
- Present uncomfortable, realistic scenario
- Wait for user response
- Score across 5 dimensions (Constraint Discovery, Systematic Thinking, Risk Assessment, AI Tool Usage, Communication Clarity)
- Provide one strength, one improvement area, do-over variant, and expert-level response for calibration
