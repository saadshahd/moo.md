Use the career skill's Executive Communication Evaluation workflow exactly as written in `references/eval-comm.md`.

Gather the communication to evaluate. Score against 5 dimensions:
- Bottom Line Up Front (BLUF)
- Ask Clarity
- Context Economy
- Risk/Blocker Surfacing
- Scannability

For each dimension: quote evidence, justify score, suggest edit to raise by 1 point.
Include time-to-comprehension estimate and BLUF-optimized rewrite of opening.
