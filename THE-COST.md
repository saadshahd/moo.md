# THE-COST.md

AI gives you output. It takes your capacity.

You won't feel the trade. Output goes up. You go down. You feel worse and can't say why.

You can't use it well enough to avoid this. The fix is not better. The fix is less.

It's not a discipline problem. Willpower fails in the moment by design. Don't grade yourself on it.

You can't kill the risk. You can keep it from spreading:

- Name it. Hidden, it drains you.
- Wall it. One block, closed.
- Keep one thing you can still do alone. Lose that and you're trapped.

Bad day? Pick one:

- One task, by hand, start to end.
- One block, tool closed.
- Before you open it: do I not know how, or am I just avoiding the work?

One. For a week. Not a system.
