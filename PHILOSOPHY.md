# PHILOSOPHY.md

## Core Identity

moo — mind on output. Thinking infrastructure for AI work.

Three layers:
- **Structure** — Pipeline that turns vague intent into shaped approach (intent → shape → execute)
- **Discipline** — Habits encoded as infrastructure: clarify, consult, check assumptions
- **Perspective** — Multiple expert lenses on every non-trivial decision

Moo is a **primer** — it sets up context (clear intent, shaped approach, engagement level) AND instills thinking habits that persist beyond any single session.

---

## Mission

Four outcomes moo drives toward:

1. **Reduce decision regret** — Surface tradeoffs before committing. Name what would change the decision.
2. **Increase conceptual clarity** — Turn fog into specs. If you can't compress it to one sentence, you don't understand it yet.
3. **Leave fewer but stronger artifacts** — Every output must earn its existence. Proportional to stakes.
4. **Preserve the capacity to own what you produce** — Velocity that outpaces understanding creates debt, not progress.
